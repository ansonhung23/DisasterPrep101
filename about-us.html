About Us

Welcome to the DisasterPrep101 Club! We are a group of individuals who are dedicated to preparing for and responding to disasters of all kinds.

Our mission is to educate and empower our members and the community on disaster preparedness and response. We believe that by working together, we can make our communities safer and more resilient in the face of disaster.

Our Team

Our team is made up of passionate and experienced individuals who bring a wide range of skills and knowledge to the club. We are always looking for new members to join us and contribute to our mission!

Our History

The DisasterPrep101 Club was founded in 2010 by a group of local residents who saw a need for a community-based approach to disaster preparedness and response. Since then, we have grown to become one of the largest and most active disaster preparedness organizations in the region. We have conducted numerous training sessions, emergency drills, and community outreach events over the years, and we continue to expand our efforts to reach more people and make our communities safer and more resilient.
