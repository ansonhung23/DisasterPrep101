Past Activities

At DisasterPrep101 Club, we hold a variety of activities aimed at fostering a culture of preparedness and resilience. Here are some of our past activities:

Activity 1:
Discussing realistic portrayals of disaster in media

This activity involved a detailed analysis and discussion of how disasters are portrayed in different media, including novels, movies, and anime broadcasts. We examined the accuracy of these portrayals in reflecting real-life contexts.

Activity 2:
2011 Japan Earthquake and Tsunami

This activity centered around an in-depth examination of the significant disaster of the 2011 Japan Earthquake and Tsunami. We discussed its impacts, the response efforts, and the lessons learned from this event.
